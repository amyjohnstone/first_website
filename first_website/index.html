About


This website is aimed at an audience with an interest in learning about what exactly is in the night sky. Someone who watched Neil deGrasse Tyson’s ‘COSMOS: A Spacetime Odyssey' and thought Ooh that looks cool but didn’t pursue a career in astrophysics because they realised how much maths was involved. This website is for you.

Cool Space Facts delivers the basics of astronomy. Our aim is to educate adults in an interesting way. We hope you enjoy using this website as much as we enjoyed creating it!

Did you know...?

Saturn can float on water.

The density of the planet Saturn (0.687 g/cm3) is less than the density of water, thus it would float!.
