Mercury or Moon?

This is the cratered surface of Mercury

Mercury


The smallest and hottest of the four terrestrial (i.e. inner rocky) planets, Mercury is first in line in the 3 billion-mile-long queue that is our solar system. It only takes Mercury 87 days to complete one orbit around the Sun.


Mercury resembles the Moon in the fact that it is covered with craters.


It is the least understood of the terrestrial planets.
