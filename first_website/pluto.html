Pluto


Last but not least is the ex-planet Pluto. Pluto lost its planetary status in 2006 and was reclassified as a dwarf planet. Pluto has 5 moons despite being smaller than our Moon! It takes Pluto almost 250 years to complete one orbit around the Sun.
